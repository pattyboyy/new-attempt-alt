Add Base to Metamask

To begin, you'll need to add the Base network to your Metamask wallet. Follow these steps:

  1. Open Metamask and click on the network dropdown.
  2. Select "Custom RPC" and enter the following details:
    • Network Name: Base
    • New RPC URL: https://base.example.com
    • Chain ID: 1234
    • Symbol: BASE
    • Block Explorer URL: https://explorer.base.example.com
  3. Click "Save" to add the Base network.

Load up ETH

Next, you'll need to have some ETH in your Metamask wallet to cover gas fees for transactions on the Base network.

You can purchase ETH from various exchanges or directly within the Metamask app using a debit/credit card or bank transfer.

Buy $COCK

With ETH in your wallet and the Base network configured, you're ready to purchase $COCK tokens.

  1. Visit the Base DEX and connect your Metamask wallet.
  2. Select the $COCK token from the list of available assets.
  3. Enter the amount of $COCK you wish to purchase and confirm the transaction.

Add $COCK to Wallet

After your purchase is complete, you'll need to add the $COCK token to your Metamask wallet to view your balance.

  1. In Metamask, click on "Import Tokens".
  2. Paste the $COCK contract address: 0x123456789abcdef...
  3. The $COCK token should now appear in your wallet with your balance.
